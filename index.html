Meet Your Mechanic

Tam Luong — 20+ Years Fixing Mankato's Cars

Tam is a South Central College-trained mechanic with over 20 years of experience working on cars in Minnesota. Before opening A & T, he owned the BP Quick Stop on Madison Avenue — so if you've been in Mankato a while, there's a good chance you already know him.

Customers keep coming back for one reason above all: honesty. Tam only recommends the repairs your car actually needs, explains what's wrong in plain language, and backs it up with fair, transparent pricing — which is why so many of his customers have been with him for over a decade.
